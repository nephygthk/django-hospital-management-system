Services

Excellent Medical Services

Emergency Care

Emergency care (AEC) service is available. You will be assessed, diagnosed and treated as an outpatient. We can ensure that any care you need is started quickly with easy, swift access to specialist clinicians and a range of diagnostic tests.

Operation & Surgery

All consultant surgeons perform laparoscopic/robotic (keyhole) surgery for most colorectal disorders. All consultant surgeons also perform the full range of general surgical procedures and manage emergency general surgery and colorectal surgery admissions to the hospital.

Outdoor Checkup

We offer a wide range of specialist and general hospital services at our two main hospital sites. Our outpatient services are for patients referred to us by clinics at our hospitals and, at our site only, patients referred to us by their GPs.

Ambulance Service

The ambulatory emergency care (AEC) service is available and offers high quality same-day emergency care (SDEC). We aim to ensure that nobody stays in hospital overnight if they are able to go home safely.

Children’s outpatients

Our specialties across both sites include cancer, neurology, ENT, ophthalmology, orthopaedics, endocrinology, cardiology and many more. We offer a blood test service within the department for children and young people of all ages attending our clinics. We also offer this service to children who have been referred by their GPs—this is by appointment only.

Blood Testing

Our phlebotomy services use blood tests to assess the general health of our patients, confirm the presence of a bacterial or viral infection, see how well organs are functioning and screen for genetic conditions.
